Solar System


Our Solar System comprises 8 planets, approximately 170 natural planetary satellites, and countless asteroids, meteorites, and comets.The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it.It was formed about 4.6 billion years ago when a dense region of a molecular cloud collapsed, forming the Sun and a protoplanetary disc.
